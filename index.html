About Me

I am an educational technology expert and technology‑enhanced instructor with extensive experience designing and delivering digital learning solutions for higher education and corporate training. As a PhD candidate and content creator, I apply research‑informed strategies to develop engaging courses and training programmes that leverage artificial intelligence and innovative instructional design.

With a passion for faculty development and leadership, I support educators and instructional designers in integrating digital tools into their practice, fostering inclusive and effective learning experiences. My work spans curriculum development, AI‑driven teaching consultancy, and strategic innovation management.

Services

  • Digital Content Development: Design and produce custom e‑learning modules, interactive simulations and multimedia courses tailored to your organisation’s needs.
  • AI‑Driven Teaching Consultancy: Integrate artificial intelligence into curriculum design to personalise learning pathways and enhance student engagement.
  • Training Programme Design & Delivery: Develop comprehensive training programmes for educators and corporate teams, including needs assessments, curriculum planning and workshop facilitation.
  • Research & Academic Writing: Provide expertise in educational research, academic paper writing and publication support, ensuring evidence‑based practice.
  • Innovation Management: Guide organisations in adopting innovative technologies and methodologies for sustainable digital transformation.
